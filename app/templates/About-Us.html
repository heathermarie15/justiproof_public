How we are unique

Heather fought against workplace discrimination for 2+ years. She remained employed at the same workplace for 19 months, through which she constantly documented incidents and captured evidence. 

What we do

Not only is it time consuming to capture incidents, but it becomes increasingly difficult to succinctly summarize what you've endured and why you know it is discrimination. 

What we believe

Every employee has the right to work in a fair and equitable workplace. When companies fail to live up to that standard, tools should exist to help employees stand up justice and make their voice heard.  

Our Mission

Empower employees to protect their rights by providing the tools and support they need to document, prove, and combat workplace discrimination. 

Our Team

Heather Wallander

Chief Executive Officer

Prior to raising concerns about discrimination, I thrived in my career and I loved what I did. That was taken from me, in no small part, because of how long I felt alone and lost, struggling to understand what was happening, why it was happening and how I could get it to stop. Over the course of 19 months a battle against one person who discriminated against me turned into a battle against an entire system who failed to protect me . In the process it forever altered who I am. 

With justiproof,  I want to equip you with a tool that confirms it's discrimination early, so you can decide the next best steps for you. If that step involves fighting back against the injustice within a broken system, we've designed it to help you do that too.

Shawn Cochran

Chief Technical Officer

As Heather's husband, I witnessed every day and every struggle of her 19-month battle against discrimination.  I watched as she spent countless hours documenting incidents, capturing evidence, and organizing timelines.

As she has spoken out, it's become clear that she is not alone in this fight and with so many in this situation there is an urgent need for a tool that makes it easier for employees to fight back.

With justiproof, I want to help make the process easier, more efficient, and accessible to everyone. Our goal is to enable  employees to  protect themselves without documentation having to consume their lives.
